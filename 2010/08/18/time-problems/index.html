Time Problems

Aug 18th, 2010

Fixing time problems is probably one of the most satisfying things to fix programmatically for me. It is one of those things where you may spend ages stabbing in the dark with different time zones, formats, etc etc. - but there it really is satisfying when you do get it right and what you see in your code is what you see on your clock. Weird, but true.
